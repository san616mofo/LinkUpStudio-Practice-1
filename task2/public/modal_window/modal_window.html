EMERGENCY SITUATIONS
FIRE OR SMOKE DETECTORS
Outside each apartment and throughout the hotel there are smoke detectors that will automatically commence the fire evacuation procedure for this hotel if activated.  They will also automatically alert the Fire Brigade to attend and once the alarm has been triggered this process cannot be stopped.

Inside your apartment there is also a smoke detector that will notify you in the case of smoke, this detector is not linked through to our fire panel.  The other Fire Suppression Systems in your room are activated by heat (such as detectors and sprinklers) and are also linked directly through to our Fire Panel and the Fire Brigade.

However, if you detect a fire, raise the alarm and contact reception by dialling 9.\nEvery minute counts.  An emergency plan is located on the back of the room door.  A call out fee may apply if the fire alarm is activated.

PREVENTION
Please take the time to familiarise yourself with the location of your room and the location of the nearest fire exit on your floor.  Please refer to the diagram located on the back of the room door for information and directions.\nIn the unlikely event that there is a fire or an emergency in the hotel:
1. Remain calm.
2. Inform Reception by dialling 9.
3. Listen out for the evacuation tones and follow any audible announcement.
4. Exit the building calmly using the closest Fire Exit stairway - do not use the elevators until advised it is safe to do so.
5. If you have a physical condition that might impair your ability to either detect an alarm or evacuate via the stairway, please notify Reception by dialling 9.
